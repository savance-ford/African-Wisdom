AfricanWisdom Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2/18/2026

AfricanWisdom (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

1. Information We Collect

AfricanWisdom does not collect, store, or transmit personal information.

  • No account required
  • No name, email, phone number, or location collection
  • No analytics tracking (at this time)

2. Local Data Storage

The App stores bookmarks locally on your device using local storage. This data stays on your device and is not sent to the developer.

3. Sharing Content

When you use the Share feature, the App uses your device’s built-in sharing tools. Any content you share is controlled by you and handled by the app or service you choose.

4. Third-Party Services

AfricanWisdom does not currently use third-party advertising or analytics services. If these services are added in the future, this policy will be updated.

5. Children’s Privacy

AfricanWisdom does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if new features are added. Updates will be reflected by changing the effective date above.
